v10.4 Proposed Changes

Core Philosophy: Data-Driven Recalibration

v10.3 was built on ELM v3 calibration data (865 windows) and CG alignment data (719 trades). Now we have 279 resolved TRADE decisions with ground truth. The data tells a different story than the model assumed.

Change 1: Regime-Specific Offset Limits

NEW

Instead of a flat V10_MIN_EVAL_OFFSET=180, use per-regime limits based on the heatmap:

v10.3 (current):
V10_MIN_EVAL_OFFSET=180 (all regimes)
→
v10.4 (proposed):
TRANSITION: 120 (87.5% WR at T60-100, drops to 61.5% at T141+)
NORMAL: 180 (42.9% WR at T60-100, 92% at T141+)
CASCADE: 180 (flat 75% across all zones)
Regimev10.3 Limitv10.4 LimitRationale
TRANSITION180120Best WR (87.5%) is at T60-100. Late TRANSITION entries (T141+) drop to 61-69%.
NORMAL180180T141-180 = 92% WR. Keep current. But add minimum: don't enter at T60-100 (42.9%).
CASCADE180180Flat ~75% across all zones. No change needed.
Impact: Unblocks TRANSITION entries at T-100..120 (currently blocked), while avoiding the late TRANSITION zone where WR drops. Also adds a minimum offset for NORMAL (block T60-100).

Change 2: Increase DOWN Penalty

CHANGE
v10.3: V10_DOWN_PENALTY=0.03
→
v10.4: V10_DOWN_PENALTY=0.05

Direction gap is 8.6pp (UP=80.7%, DOWN=72.1%) on N=279. Current +0.03 is insufficient — data shows +0.05 better fits the observed gap.

Exception: NORMAL DOWN is 87.2% WR — best of any bucket. Consider direction-aware regime thresholds (see Change 4).

Change 3: TRANSITION Threshold Increase

CHANGE
v10.3: V10_TRANSITION_MIN_P=0.70
→
v10.4: V10_TRANSITION_MIN_P=0.75

TRANSITION is 71.7% WR overall — lowest regime. At N=159 this is the highest-volume bucket. Raising the bar from 0.70 to 0.75 would cut the weakest TRANSITION trades.

Change 4: Delta Magnitude Gate

NEW

Large deltas (>0.10%) have 70.1% WR vs 78-79% for moderate deltas. Add a penalty or soft gate:

ParameterValueEffect
V10_LARGE_DELTA_THRESHOLD0.10%When |delta| exceeds this...
V10_LARGE_DELTA_PENALTY+0.03...raise threshold by 0.03

Rationale: extreme moves are noisy (whipsaws, flash crashes). A +0.03 penalty requires higher ELM confidence to trade into large moves.

Change 5: NORMAL Early Entry Block

NEW

NORMAL at T60-100 is 42.9% WR (3W/4L, N=7). Add a minimum offset for NORMAL regime:

V10_NORMAL_MIN_OFFSET=100

Small sample but extreme underperformance. Low risk to block — only affects ~3% of NORMAL entries. The NORMAL regime needs time for the delta to stabilize.

Change 6: Keep CG Taker Gate OFF

KEEP

The audit proved CG taker data was neutral-to-aligned on all 4 non-offset losses. Enabling the hard taker gate would NOT have blocked any additional losses beyond offset. The 719-trade alignment dataset may not generalize to the current market regime.

Recommendation: Keep V10_CG_TAKER_GATE=false. Continue collecting shadow data. Re-evaluate after 100+ v10.3 resolved trades with CG data attached.

Change 7: Fix Fill Rate (Highest Priority)

CRITICAL

80% of orders expire unfilled. This is a bigger problem than any gate tuning. Proposed fixes:

FixImpactRisk
Aggressive pricing: bid at ask-1 tick instead of mid2-3x fill rate improvementWorse average entry price (-$0.01/share)

At current fill rate (19.4%), we're seeing ~4 fills/day. Doubling fill rate to 40% would double trade volume and proportionally increase daily PnL from ~$5 to ~$10 at 72.7% WR.
