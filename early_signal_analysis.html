Additional Data Sources to Explore

  • Order Book Depth (L2) — Bid/ask imbalance at key levels is a strong leading indicator. Available via Binance WebSocket depth stream. Likely adds 3–5% accuracy.
  • Funding Rates (CoinGlass/Binance Futures) — Extreme funding predicts reversion. Useful for longer-horizon signals.
  • Liquidation Heatmaps (CoinGlass) — Large liquidation clusters act as price magnets. API: coinglass.com
  • Perpetual vs Spot Premium — Futures basis divergence predicts short-term direction. Available from Binance BTCUSDT perps.
  • Aggressor Volume (Trade Stream) — Real-time taker flow is more granular than 1-min OHLCV. Binance websocket: @trade

Implementation Notes

  • Use Binance WebSocket for real-time aggressor volume (more accurate than klines tbvol)
  • At T-60s, window_delta and taker_buy_ratio are the most reliable OHLCV signals
  • Combined signals using majority vote show marginal improvement — consider weighted voting
  • Consider training a lightweight classifier (logistic regression) on all features combined
  • Backtest with real Polymarket market prices to validate EV estimates
